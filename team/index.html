Behind the scenes

Stream was built on years of experience with creating streams for high-traffic websites. We enable you to quickly build scalable newsfeeds and activity streams.

Thierry Schellenbach

CEO

Thierry graduated cum laude with honours from Erasmus university with a degree in business. At the age of 14, he started his first profitable site and discovered a passion for technology. Before founding Stream with Tommaso, he co-founded Fashiolista, which grew to be one of the world's largest social networks for fashion. He's the author of several widely used Python packages including Stream-Framework and Django-facebook and has more than 5 years of experience managing technology companies.

LinkedIn Twitter

Tommaso Barbugli

CTO

Tommaso is an enthusiastic software developer. He discovered programming at a young age using an old Amstrad CPC464, since then he has continued to learn about and create new programs. While studying at high school he started focusing on web development and at age 19, he started his own software company after finishing his studies. Before founding Stream he worked together with Thierry at Fashiolista as Lead Engineer for a few years. He is the co-author of the open source Stream-Framework, Cassandra Snapshotter and several other libraries.
